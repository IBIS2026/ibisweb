Meet IBIS

A physical interface for GenAI in classrooms.

For Students

Students gain distraction-less interaction with GenAI, focusing purely on learning without the typical screen-based interruptions.

For Teachers

Teachers gain insight into student learning via a comprehensive dashboard and diagnostics, featuring frequently asked questions and a summary of the main problems the class is facing.

Handmade Hard & Software

IBIS doesn't just live in the cloud—it exists physically in the learning space. Housed in a custom-designed orange chassis, the units provide a tangible, approachable presence.
